Blessing Israel

Doctor of Mathematics | Founder, Lead Strategist & Academic Director

Blessing Israel is an accomplished educator, Mathematics specialist, TRCN-licensed teacher, and the Founder, Lead Strategist & Academic Director of The Engraced Academy of Excellence, a CAC-registered educational institution committed to delivering premium, personalised, and results-driven education to learners in Nigeria and across the globe.

Widely known as the Doctor of Mathematics, Blessing Israel is recognised for her unique ability to identify the root causes of academic struggles, design targeted intervention strategies, restore confidence, and help learners achieve measurable academic transformation.

Her passion for Mathematics was ignited immediately after secondary school when she encountered an exceptional teacher whose approach transformed her perspective and deepened her love for the subject. That encounter inspired a lifelong commitment to making Mathematics engaging, practical, and accessible to learners of every ability.

FOUNDATIONAL TEACHING JOURNEY

From Purpose to Premium Academic Intervention

In 2007, during her National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) programme, she returned to her alma mater, Federal Government College, Enugu (FGCE), where she served as a corps member and taught Chemistry.

While at FGCE, she also established and led extra tutorial classes for both Chemistry and Mathematics students. These classes gave learners additional support, helped them strengthen difficult concepts, build confidence, and improve their academic performance.

In addition to her classroom and tutorial impact, she established a standard Science Club in a school, creating opportunities for learners to explore Science beyond the textbook through practical activities, curiosity-driven learning, and hands-on engagement.

That early experience shaped her belief that learners do not simply need more teaching; they need the right diagnosis, the right intervention, and the right support system.

Private Academic Coaching

Beyond her formal classroom experience, Blessing Israel has also provided private academic coaching at postgraduate level. In 2014, while pursuing her Master of Science (M.Sc.) programme, she coached postgraduate students in Medical Statistics at the Department of Pharmaceutical Chemistry, University of Lagos. All the postgraduate students in her tutorial class passed, and she graduated with a Distinction.

In 2020, while pursuing her Postgraduate Diploma in Education (PGDE), she also coached fellow postgraduate students in Statistical Methods in Education, further strengthening her reputation for simplifying complex quantitative courses with clarity, structure, and confidence.

Over the years, she has successfully taught, coached, and mentored learners across multiple educational systems and age groups, preparing students for Common Entrance, BECE, WAEC, NECO, JAMB, IGCSE, GCSE, SAT, and other national and international examinations.

As the visionary behind The Engraced Academy of Excellence, she has developed an educational model that goes beyond conventional tutoring. Every learner begins with a careful assessment of strengths, weaknesses, and learning gaps, allowing each intervention to be personalised to the learner’s unique needs. This diagnostic approach ensures that instruction is focused, measurable, and transformational.

RAISING YOUNG SCIENTISTS

Practical Science from the Comfort of Home

Her passion for practical Science did not end in the physical classroom. Today, she continues that vision through The Engraced Academy’s robust online Science Club, where learners conduct exciting experiments, participate in guided STEM activities, and experience practical Science from the comfort of their homes.

Through this platform, learners are encouraged to ask questions, investigate ideas, build curiosity, and develop the confidence of young scientists.
